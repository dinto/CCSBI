Citizen Science workshop - Vivekananda College, Kizhoor

August 16, 2022

Centre for Citizen Science and Biodiversity Informatics (CCSBI) organised a workshop on ‘Biodiversity assessment and introduction to ‘iNaturalist’ a citizen science platform’. This workshop was arranged in collaboration with the Nature club, Sree Vivekananda College Kizhoor, Kunnamkulam. And aimed at students and faculties who were interested in participating and organising biodiversity assessments in the campus. The workshop kicked off with a brief introduction to the ‘iNaturalist’ platform and the mobile application. Know More
