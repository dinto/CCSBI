Taxonomy Workshops

Hands-on workshops on taxonomy will be carried out at the Kerala Forest Research Institute. Workshops will be focusing on capacitating young conservationists and enthusiasts on proper identification of selected taxonomic groups. Under expert guidance, participants will be encouraged to identify and scrutinise crowdsourced data available on open access platforms like iNaturalist as the hands on training for this program.



Data management, analysis and visualisation

If we are unable to interpret the data, all the effort we put into data collection will be in vain. So before designing a project you should have a clear idea of what kind of data you should be collecting, how it should be managed, what kind of output you are expecting and what analysis should be carried out to interpret the data. This session will be covering the basics of biostatistics and introduction to softwares.
